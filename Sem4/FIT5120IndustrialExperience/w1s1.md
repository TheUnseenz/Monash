# Impact of Air Pollution on Elderly Health in Urban Malaysia
## Problem Statement
Air pollution causes notable health risks to the elderly in urban Malaysia, which not just directly harms their respiratory health, but also indirectly impairs their quality of life via stress, anxiety and having to take mitigating actions.

## Onboarding Persona 
**Gender:** Male
**Age:** 70  
**Status:** Retired
**Background:** Lives in a quiet neighbourhood, children have all moved out

### Goals
- Wants to live a quiet, simple, retirement
- Wants to reduce body pains and maintain quality of life
- Wants to have people to talk to

### Frustrations  
- Air pollution makes it unpleasant to be outdoors
- Doesn't want to risk getting respiratory problems
- Other friends also don't want to meet up so often due to risk of health problems

### Behaviors
- Likes to go out for simple morning exercises at the park
- Likes to meet up with friends at local hawker stalls

### Needs
- To be able to go out to the park
- To be able to meet others outdoors
